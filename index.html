What to Look for in an Omaha Roofing Contractor

Questions to Ask Contractors

When vetting a potential contractor, ask these simple questions. If they can't be answered easily, continue your search.

  • ✓Can I see your contractor's license?
  • ✓Do you have general liability and workers' comp insurance?
  • ✓Where is your office and headquarters?
  • ✓What is the duration of your warranty?
  • ✓What training have your installers completed?

Questions They Should Ask You

If a contractor isn't asking questions like these, they may not have your best interest in mind.

  • ✓Have you dealt with an insurance claim before?
  • ✓What's most important to you about this project?
  • ✓Do you currently have any leaks?
  • ✓How involved do you like to be — every step, or hands-off?
  • ✓Is there other storm damage that needs to be reported?

If integrity and quality workmanship matter to you, you're in the right place.
